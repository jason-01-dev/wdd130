Kinshasa | DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF THE CONGO

Kinshasa is the capital and largest city of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. It is a vibrant city known for its rich culture, music, and history. Kinshasa is located on the Congo River, directly across from Brazzaville, the capital of the Republic of the Congo. The city is a major hub for commerce, art, and education in Central Africa.
